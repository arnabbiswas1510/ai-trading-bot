    fix: reconcile_with_ibkr() use ib.portfolio() instead of ib.positions()
    
    ib.positions() is a push-subscription that may be empty if the subscription
    hasn't fired yet in the current gateway session, causing false 'in sync'
    results when Supabase is empty but IBKR holds real positions.
    
    ib.portfolio() is populated synchronously on IB connection and is always
    accurate. Also fixes avgCost -> averageCost (PortfolioItem attribute).
